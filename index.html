Human Trafficking Data Observatory

Southern Africa’s first public investigation into human trafficking

The Human Trafficking Data Observatory is an investigative data project into modern slavery in southern Africa. We use data from social media and internet forums to track how traffickers use online communities to traffick vulnerable people, especially women and children.

We’re also building an information library right here. This is where reliable demographic data and statistics on human trafficking can easily be found. Our team of researchers and forensic data investigators are building this library in partnership with NGOs and experts working in the anti-trafficking movement.
